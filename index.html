Course outline

Te Pā Tūwatawata is used here as the anchor case study for teaching AI, Māori data sovereignty, governance, and digital futures from a Māori perspective.

Six modules

1. Whakapapa o te raraunga

The whakapapa of data.

2. Te Pā Tūwatawata hei tauira

Te Pā Tūwatawata as a model.

3. AI me te raupatu matihiko

AI and digital extraction.

4. Tikanga, ture, mana whakahaere

Tikanga, law, governance.

5. Hoahoa tika

Designing ethical systems.

6. He anamata rangatira

Sovereign digital futures.

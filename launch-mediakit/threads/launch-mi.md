I tēnei rā ka whakarewahia [te-pa.org](https://te-pa.org) — he pae mātauranga utukore, tūwhera, mō te mana raraunga me te mārama AI.

## He aha ai a Te Pā

Ko te AI ko te raupatu hou. Ka whakangungua ngā tauira AI ki ngā reo, ngā kanohi, ngā waiata, ngā kōrero Māori e kore he whakaaetanga. He rautau anō, he kaupapa anō, engari he raupatu tonu. Ko Te Pā Tūwatawata te whakautu mātauranga.

He Tarahiti Atawhai mātou, ki Te Pā Hawakawaka, ki Aotearoa, e hanga ana i te hanganga mātauranga tūwhera mō te mana raraunga taketake, te mōhio AI, me ngā pūnaha mātauranga koretake — i runga i ngā tikanga o ngā tohu ahurea o Te Ao Māori, Sāmoa, Te Moana-nui-a-Kiwa, Guaraní, Shipibo, Guna, Kayapó, Yanomami, Fiti, Tonga, me Amazon.

## Ka aha i tēnei rā

- **30 tohu ahurea** ki ngā ahurea 11 — PNG, SVG, miihini AI hoki
- **6 ngā kete whakaako utukore** — Reo Pākehā, Te Reo Māori, Português, Avañe'ẽ, Gagana Samoa, Tikanga Arapi
- **6 ngā kōwae utukore** — reorua, e tau ana ki Te Tiriti o Waitangi, ki ngā tikanga Māori, ki ngā mātāpono CARE me OCAP
- **API tūmatanui mō te pānui** — 100 inoi ia haora, kāore he kī
- **Raihana CC BY-NC-SA 4.0** — utukore ki te whakamahi, whakamāori, wāwāhi, hau-māhau

## Karangatanga

Pānuihia ngā kōwae. Tāhia ngā pānui. Piritia ngā piriti. Wāwāhia te pae. Whakamāoritia ki tō reo. Mauria ki tō akomanga. te-pa.org — i hangaia whakapapa-tuatahi, kua reri mō ngā hoa-mahi.

# Te Pā Tūwatawata — Public Launch Announcement

> **Language:** English · **Audience:** International + Aotearoa default

Today we launch [te-pa.org](https://te-pa.org) — a free, open educational platform for indigenous data sovereignty and AI literacy.

## Why Te Pā

AI is the latest form of raupatu. Tech companies train models on Māori voices, faces, waiata, and stories without consent — different century, same logic. Te Pā Tūwatawata is the educational answer.

We are an open educational kaupapa published from Ōtepoti Dunedin (Port Chalmers), Aotearoa New Zealand. A charitable trust is being established — expressions of interest open for founding board / kaitiaki seats. We build open educational infrastructure for indigenous data sovereignty, AI literacy, and decolonial knowledge systems — rooted in the cultural motif traditions of Māori, Samoan, Pacific, Guaraní, Shipibo, Guna, Kayapó, Yanomami, Fijian, Tongan, and Amazonian peoples.

## What's live today

- **30 cultural motifs** across 11 cultures — full PNG, SVG, and activist meme artwork
- **6 free teaching kits** — English, Te Reo Māori, Português, Avañe'ẽ, Gagana Samoa, Arabic
- **6 free modules** — bilingual, grounded in Te Tiriti o Waitangi, tikanga Māori, and the CARE/OCAP indigenous-data principles
- **Public read-only API** — 100 requests/hour, no key required
- **CC BY-NC-SA 4.0 licence** — free to use, translate, fork, and self-host

## Call to action

Read the modules. Print the posters. Stick the stickers. Fork the repo. Translate it into your reo. Bring it into your classroom. te-pa.org — built whakapapa-first, partner-ready.
